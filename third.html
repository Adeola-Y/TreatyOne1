Treaty One

We acknowledge being on Treaty One territory and commit to moving forward in partnership with Indigenous communities towards reconciliation. We recognize that we are gathered on Treaty 1 Territory and that Manitoba is located on the Treaty Territories and ancestral lands of the Anishinaabeg, Anishininewuk, Dakota Oyate, Denesuline, and Nehethowuk Nations. *Source For Land Acknowledgment*
